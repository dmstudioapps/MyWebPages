Privacy Policy for To-Do List - Task Tracker

Last updated: 26 December 2025

Developer: Data & Mind Studio

To-Do List - Task Tracker (“the App”) is developed by Data & Mind Studio. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not directly collect, store, or transmit personal information. All tasks, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Advertisements

The App displays bottom banner advertisements using third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob).

These services may automatically collect limited information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type and operating system
  • Approximate (non-precise) location

This data is collected by the ad provider, not by us, and is governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. In-App Purchases (Remove Ads)

The App offers a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase to remove advertisements.

  • Payments are processed securely by Google Play
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device

4. Local Device Storage

All user-created data remains on your device only. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete this data.

5. Notifications

The App uses device-based local notifications to remind you of tasks. No notification data is transmitted externally.

6. No Accounts or Login

The App does not require user registration, login, or cloud synchronization.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We rely on your device’s operating system for data protection. You are responsible for securing your device and backups.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App or its Play Store listing.
