Privacy Policy — Daily Reflection Journal

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Daily Reflection Journal (the “App”) is developed and maintained by DM Studio ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. The App does not require an account or any online login.

  • We do not collect names, emails, or contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • We do not transmit your journal entries or moods to any server.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All reflections, moods, and settings are stored locally on your device’s storage. This means:

  • Only you can access your journal data on your device.
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all saved reflections from the device.
  • You can manually back up or export your data (for example, to a PDF file).

3. Permissions Used

The App requests minimal permissions, strictly for functionality:

  • Notifications – used to remind you to add reflections (morning and evening reminders).
  • Storage Access – used only for exporting or importing your journal data when you explicitly choose to do so.

No other permissions are requested or used.

4. Internet & Connectivity

The App is designed to work entirely offline. We do not send your data to any servers. Should future versions introduce optional network features, we will:

  • Clearly communicate what the feature does;
  • Request your explicit consent before enabling any network-related functionality.

5. Your Control

You are in control of your data. You can:

  • Export or delete your reflections at any time from within the App.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all stored data from your device.

We do not retain any copies of your data once deleted from your device.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: dmstudio.apps@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the App after changes indicates you accept the updated Policy.

Summary: 100% offline journaling — no data collection, no ads, and your reflections stay on your device.
