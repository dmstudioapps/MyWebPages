Privacy Policy for To Do Tracker

Last updated: 12 December 2025

Developer: dmstudio

To Do Tracker (“the App”) is developed by dmstudio. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All data remains on your device.

2. Local Device Storage

All tasks, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling deletes this data.

3. Notifications

The App uses device-based local notifications. No data is sent externally.

4. No Internet Communication

The App does not use the internet for data transfer. No accounts, analytics, or ads are used.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services or SDKs.

6. Children’s Privacy

No personal data is collected from any user, including children.

7. Security

Users are responsible for securing their device and backups.

8. Changes

Updates to this policy will be reflected on our website or Play Store listing.
