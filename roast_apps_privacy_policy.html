Information We Collect

Google Sign-In

When you sign in using Google, we collect only your email address for authentication and account identification purposes. Google Sign-In is optional.

We do not collect your name, contacts, profile photo, or any other personal information.

Camera Permission

Our apps may request access to your device’s camera to enable image-based features such as generating roasts or related content.

  • Images are processed locally or temporarily
  • Images are not stored on our servers
  • Images are not shared with third parties
  • Images are not used for tracking or identification

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data such as app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to improve app performance and user experience.

Advertising Data

Free users may see advertisements. Advertising partners may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide core app functionality
  • Improve app stability and performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • We do not store user-generated images or content
  • We store only your email address for login purposes
  • Authentication data is protected using reasonable security measures

Delete Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated data at any time.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Google sign-in email address
  • Any authentication records linked to your account

We do not retain images or generated content, as this data is never stored.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion in one of the following ways:

  • In-app: Settings → Delete Account
  • By email: Send a request from your registered email address

Email: dmstudio.apps@gmail.com

Subject: Delete My Account

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed through Google Play. We do not store your payment or billing information on our servers.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
